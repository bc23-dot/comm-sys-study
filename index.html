第1课 Lecture 1

📋 通信基础

Fundamentals of Communication
📖 概览 Overview

通信是从源端到目的端交换信息的过程。本课介绍通信系统的基本组成、信号类型、信道特性及信号损伤。

Communication is the process of exchanging information from a source to a destination. This lecture introduces the basic components of communication systems, signal types, channel characteristics, and signal impairments.

通信系统要素 Elements of a Communication System
信息源 — 产生待传输的消息Information Source — Produces the message to be transmitted
输入换能器 — 将一种能量转换为另一种（如麦克风：声→电）Input Transducer — Converts one form of energy to another (e.g. microphone: acoustic → electrical)
发射机 — 对信号进行调制和放大Transmitter — Modulates and amplifies the signal
信道 — 信息从发射机到接收机的传输媒介Channel — The medium over which information passes from transmitter to receiver
接收机 — 接收失真信号，进行解调、放大和滤波Receiver — Receives the distorted signal, demodulates, amplifies, and filters
信息接收端 — 信号的最终目的地（音频/视频输出、存储设备）Information Sink — Final destination (audio/video output, storage devices)
通信系统类型 Types of Communication Systems
单工 (Simplex) — 仅单向通信，如广播电视Simplex — One direction only, e.g. radio/TV broadcasting
半双工 (Half-Duplex) — 双向但不能同时，如对讲机Half-Duplex — Both directions but not simultaneously, e.g. walkie-talkie
全双工 (Full-Duplex) — 同时双向，如电话Full-Duplex — Simultaneous bidirectional, e.g. telephone
信道分类 Channel Classification
有线信道 — 双绞线、同轴电缆、光纤Guided/Wired — Twisted pair, coaxial cable, optical fibre
无线信道 — 无线电波自由传播Wireless/Free propagation — Radio waves through free space
电话信道：带通 300–3400 Hz，SNR ~30 dBTelephone channel: bandpass 300–3400 Hz, SNR ~30 dB
光纤优势：高带宽、低衰减、抗电磁干扰、无串扰Fibre advantages: high BW, low attenuation, immune to EMI, no crosstalk
信道损伤 Channel Impairments
衰减 (Attenuation) — 信号功率随距离降低Attenuation — Signal power decreases with distance
失真 (Distortion) — 不同频率分量传播速度不同导致信号变形Distortion — Different frequency components have different propagation speeds
噪声 (Noise) — 热噪声、感应噪声、串扰、脉冲噪声Noise — Thermal, induced, crosstalk, impulse noise
信号类型 Signal Types
模拟信号 — 时间和幅度均连续，如语音、正弦波Analogue Signal — Continuous in both time and amplitude
数字信号 — 时间连续但幅度离散，使用有限符号集Digital Signal — Continuous in time, discrete in amplitude
周期信号 — s(t+T)=s(t)，T为周期Periodic Signal — s(t+T)=s(t), T = period
非周期信号 — 不重复Aperiodic Signal — Does not repeat
分贝 (Decibel)
$$\text{dB} = 10\log_{10}\frac{P_2}{P_1}$$
信噪比 (SNR)
$$\text{SNR} = \frac{P_{\text{signal}}}{P_{\text{noise}}},\quad \text{SNR}_{\text{dB}} = 10\log_{10}(\text{SNR})$$
正弦波 (Sine Wave)
$$y(t) = A\sin(2\pi f t + \varphi)$$
周期-频率关系
$$T = \frac{1}{f},\quad f = \frac{1}{T}$$
n比特字母表大小
$$\text{Alphabet size} = 2^n$$

📊 信号与传输

Signals and Transmission
📖 概览 Overview

深入理解信号的时域与频域特性、信号运算、传输介质、数据速率限制、复用技术和网络性能指标。

Deep dive into time/frequency domain concepts, signal operations, transmission media, data rate limits, multiplexing, and network performance metrics.

时域概念 Time-Domain Concepts
连续时间信号 x(t) — 在每个时刻都有定义Continuous-time x(t) — defined at every instant of time
离散时间信号 {x[n]} — 仅在离散时刻定义Discrete-time {x[n]} — defined only at discrete instants
确定信号 — 完全可预测，如 sin(4πt)Deterministic — fully specified for all time
随机信号 — 不可预测，如热噪声、语音Random/Stochastic — unpredictable, e.g. thermal noise, speech
偶信号 x(t)=x(-t)；奇信号 x(t)=-x(-t)Even x(t)=x(-t); Odd x(t)=-x(-t)
任意信号 = 偶部 + 奇部：x(t)=[x(t)+x(-t)]/2 + [x(t)-x(-t)]/2Any signal = even + odd part
信号运算 Signal Operations
时移 x(t-t₀)：t₀>0 延迟(右移)，t₀<0 提前(左移)Time shifting x(t-t₀): delay (t₀>0) or advance (t₀<0)
时间缩放 x(at)：a>1 压缩，0<a<1 展宽Time scaling x(at): compression (a>1) or expansion (0
时移+缩放 x(at-b)：先移后缩 or 先缩后移Combined x(at-b): shift then scale, or scale then shift
频域概念 Frequency-Domain Concepts
傅里叶理论：任何周期函数 = 不同频率正弦/余弦的加权和Fourier: Any periodic function = weighted sum of sines and cosines
方波近似：f(t) = (4/π)Σ sin((2n-1)πt)/(2n-1)Square wave: f(t) = (4/π)Σ sin((2n-1)πt)/(2n-1)
频谱 (Spectrum) — 信号包含的频率范围Spectrum — range of frequencies a signal contains
绝对带宽 — 频谱总宽度；有效带宽 — 包含大部分能量的窄带Absolute BW vs Effective BW — total width vs narrow band with most energy
传输介质 Transmission Media
无线电波 3kHz–1GHz：全向、可穿墙，用于广播和移动通信Radio waves 3kHz–1GHz: omni-directional, penetrate walls
微波 1–300GHz：定向、视距传输，用于卫星和WLANMicrowaves 1–300GHz: unidirectional, line-of-sight
红外：短距离、不穿墙，如遥控器Infrared: short-range, cannot penetrate walls, remote controls
光纤：带宽可达铜线的1000倍，基于全内反射Fibre optic: up to 1000× copper BW, total internal reflection
数据速率限制 Data Rate Limits
奈奎斯特 (无噪声)：BitRate = 2B·log₂(L)Nyquist (noiseless): BitRate = 2B·log₂(L)
香农 (有噪声)：C = B·log₂(1+SNR)Shannon (noisy): C = B·log₂(1+SNR)
极噪信道：SNR→0，C=B·log₂(1)=0，无法通信Extremely noisy channel: SNR→0 → C=0, no communication possible
电话线示例：B=3000Hz, SNR=3162 → C≈34.9 kbpsTelephone line: B=3000Hz, SNR=3162 → C≈34.9 kbps
网络性能 Network Performance
吞吐量 — 实际传输速率（可能远低于理论带宽）Throughput — actual delivered data rate
延迟 = 传播时间 + 传输时间 + 排队时间 + 处理延迟Latency = propagation + transmission + queuing + processing delay
传播时间 = 距离/传播速度；传输时间 = 消息大小/带宽Propagation time = distance/speed; Transmission time = message size/BW
抖动 (Jitter) — 不同数据包延迟的差异，影响实时应用Jitter — variation in packet delay, affects real-time apps
带宽-延迟积 — 管道「容积」= 带宽 × 延迟Bandwidth-delay product — volume of the pipe = BW × delay
波长 λ = vₚ/fWavelength λ = vₚ/f
复用技术 Multiplexing
FDM 频分复用 — 将带宽分为多个子频带，模拟技术FDM — divide bandwidth into sub-bands, analog technique
TDM 时分复用 — 将时间分为时隙，数字技术TDM — divide time into slots, digital technique
SDM 空分复用 — 同频段在不同物理空间复用SDM — same frequency in physically separate zones
CDM 码分复用 — 所有用户同时同频传输，用伪随机码区分CDM — all users transmit simultaneously, separated by codes
奈奎斯特速率
$$\text{BitRate} = 2B\log_2(L)$$
香农容量
$$C = B\log_2(1+\text{SNR})$$
波长
$$\lambda = \frac{v_p}{f} = v_p T$$
延迟
$$\text{Latency} = t_{\text{prop}} + t_{\text{trans}} + t_{\text{queue}} + t_{\text{proc}}$$
吞吐量计算
$$\text{Throughput} = \frac{\text{frames/min} \times \text{bits/frame}}{60}$$

📡 模拟调制与数字调制基础

Analog & Digital Modulation Basics
📖 概览 Overview

学习为什么需要调制、三种模拟调制（AM/FM/PM）的原理、AM解调方法，以及数字调制基础（ASK/FSK/PSK）。

Learn why modulation is needed, three analog modulation techniques (AM/FM/PM), AM demodulation, and digital modulation basics (ASK/FSK/PSK).

为什么需要调制？ Why Modulate?
天线高度问题：音频f=20kHz，λ=c/f=15km，所需天线~1500m（不可行）Antenna height: for f=20kHz, λ=15km, antenna ~1500m (impossible)
信号混叠：所有音频信号在20Hz–20kHz同频段，直接传输会混在一起Signal mixing: all audio in same 20Hz–20kHz band would interfere
辐射效率低：基带音频信号在开放大气中只能传输几米Poor radiation: baseband audio travels only a few meters
实现复用：调制后可在同一链路上容纳多用户Enable multiplexing: multiple users on one link
载波信号 Carrier Signal
c(t) = Vc·cos(2π·fc·t + φ) — 三个可调参数：幅度Vc、频率fc、相位φc(t) = Vc·cos(2π·fc·t + φ) — three adjustable parameters: Vc, fc, φ
幅度调制 AM Amplitude Modulation (AM)
调制信号：am(t) = [Vc + Vm·cos(2π·fm·t)]·cos(2π·fc·t)AM signal: am(t) = [Vc + Vm·cos(2π·fm·t)]·cos(2π·fc·t)
频谱三分量：载波fc、下边带fc–fm、上边带fc+fmSpectrum: carrier fc, LSB fc–fm, USB fc+fm
AM带宽：B_AM = 2 × B_messageAM Bandwidth: B_AM = 2 × B_message
AM电台：530–1700 kHz，每站10 kHz间隔AM radio: 530–1700 kHz, 10 kHz per station
解调：包络检波器（二极管检波器）Demodulation: Envelope detector (diode detector)
频率调制 FM Frequency Modulation (FM)
瞬时频率随调制信号变化：fi(t) = fc + m(t)Instantaneous frequency: fi(t) = fc + m(t)
FM带宽：B_FM = 2(1+β)B（β通常为4）FM Bandwidth: B_FM = 2(1+β)B (β commonly 4)
FM电台：88–108 MHz，每站200 kHzFM radio: 88–108 MHz, 200 kHz per station
100个潜在FM频段，同一区域最多50个同时使用100 potential FM bands, max 50 simultaneously in one area
相位调制 PM Phase Modulation (PM)
载波相位随调制信号幅度变化，幅度和频率保持不变Carrier phase follows modulating signal amplitude; amplitude and frequency constant
PM带宽：B_PM = 2(1+β)B（窄带β≈1，宽带β≈3）PM Bandwidth: B_PM = 2(1+β)B (narrowband β≈1, wideband β≈3)
FM和PM统称为角度调制 (Angle Modulation)FM and PM together: Angle Modulation
数字调制基础 Digital Modulation Basics
ASK 振幅键控：改变载波幅度，对噪声最敏感ASK: vary amplitude; most susceptible to noise
FSK 频移键控：改变载波频率，抗噪优于ASKFSK: vary frequency; better than ASK
PSK 相移键控：改变载波相位，性能最佳PSK: vary phase; best performance
可靠性排序：PSK > FSK > ASKReliability: PSK > FSK > ASK
比特率 vs 波特率：N = r × S，r = log₂(L)Bit rate vs Baud rate: N = r × S, r = log₂(L)
星座图：在二维平面上表示数字调制信号Constellation diagram: 2D representation of modulated signal
BPSK：2相位(0°,180°)；QPSK：4相位，2比特/符号BPSK: 2 phases; QPSK: 4 phases, 2 bits/symbol
AM信号 (单音)
$$am(t) = [V_c + V_m\cos(2\pi f_m t)]\cos(2\pi f_c t)$$
AM带宽
$$B_{AM} = 2B_{message}$$
FM瞬时频率
$$f_i(t) = f_c + m(t)$$
FM/PM带宽
$$B = 2(1+\beta)B_{message}$$
比特率关系
$$N = rS,\quad r = \log_2(L)$$

🔷 数字调制进阶

Advanced Digital Modulation
📖 概览 Overview

深入多进制数字调制（M-ary Modulation），理解QAM调制方案，掌握调制阶数与频谱效率的权衡。

Deep dive into M-ary digital modulation, understand QAM schemes, and learn the trade-off between modulation order and spectral efficiency.

M进制调制 M-ary Modulation
二进制调制 (L=2)：每次传1比特Binary (L=2): 1 bit per symbol
M进制调制：L = 2ʳ 个符号，每个符号代表 r 比特M-ary: L = 2ʳ symbols, r = log₂(L) bits per symbol
比特率 N = r × S = r/Ts（相同带宽下比特率更高）Bit rate N = r × S = r/Ts (higher bit rate within same BW)
M-ASK / M-FSK / M-PSK M-ASK / M-FSK / M-PSK
M-ASK：多个幅度值，每个幅度编码多比特M-ASK: multiple amplitude levels, each encodes multiple bits
M-FSK：L个不同载波频率，需谨慎选择频率以避免干扰M-FSK: L different carrier frequencies
QPSK (4-PSK)：4相位，r=2，比特率翻倍或带宽减半QPSK: 4 phases, r=2, double bit rate or halve bandwidth
8-PSK：r=3；16-PSK：r=4（相位间隔仅22.5°，易受噪声影响）8-PSK: r=3; 16-PSK: r=4 (22.5° separation, noise-sensitive)
QAM 正交幅度调制 Quadrature Amplitude Modulation (QAM)
QAM：同时改变幅度和相位 → 在星座图中增加符号间距QAM: modulate both amplitude and phase → more distance between symbols
s(t) = A_I·cos(2π·fc·t) + A_Q·sin(2π·fc·t) — 同相+正交分量s(t) = A_I·cos(2π·fc·t) + A_Q·sin(2π·fc·t) — I + Q components
4QAM = QPSK；8QAM：2幅度×4相位4QAM = QPSK; 8QAM: 2 amplitudes × 4 phases
高阶QAM：16-QAM, 64-QAM, 256-QAM（方形星座图网格）Higher-order: 16-QAM, 64-QAM, 256-QAM (square grid constellations)
64-QAM和256-QAM广泛用于数字有线电视和电缆调制解调器64-QAM & 256-QAM used in digital cable TV and cable modems
调制阶数对比 Modulation Order Comparison
BASK/BFSK/BPSK → 1 bit/symbolBASK/BFSK/BPSK → 1 bit/symbol
4-PSK/4-QAM → 2 bits/symbol4-PSK/4-QAM → 2 bits/symbol
8-PSK/8-QAM → 3 bits/symbol8-PSK/8-QAM → 3 bits/symbol
16-QAM → 4 bits/symbol16-QAM → 4 bits/symbol
64-QAM → 6 bits/symbol64-QAM → 6 bits/symbol
256-QAM → 8 bits/symbol256-QAM → 8 bits/symbol
⚠️ L越大，符号间距越小 → 对噪声越敏感⚠️ Higher L → closer symbols → more noise-sensitive
M进制符号数
$$L = 2^r,\quad r = \log_2(L)$$
奈奎斯特最大符号率
$$S_{\max} = 2B$$
QAM信号
$$s_i(t) = A_{I,i}\cos(2\pi f_c t) + A_{Q,i}\sin(2\pi f_c t)$$
M-PSK相位
$$\phi_i = \frac{2\pi}{L}(i-1) + \text{const}$$

🔢 模数转换与数数转换

A/D and D/D Conversion
📖 概览 Overview

学习PCM脉冲编码调制的三个步骤（采样、量化、编码），Delta调制，线路编码方案，以及并行/串行传输模式。

Learn PCM's three steps (sampling, quantization, encoding), Delta modulation, line coding schemes, and parallel/serial transmission modes.

PCM 脉冲编码调制 Pulse Code Modulation (PCM)
第1步：采样 — 每隔Ts秒采样一次，fs=1/TsStep 1: Sampling — sample every Ts seconds, fs=1/Ts
奈奎斯特定理：fs ≥ 2·fmax（采样率至少为最高频率的两倍）Nyquist Theorem: fs ≥ 2·fmax
第2步：量化 — 将幅度范围分为L个区间，每个高度ΔStep 2: Quantization — divide amplitude into L zones of height Δ
Δ = (Vmax–Vmin)/L；nb = log₂(L) 比特/样本Δ = (Vmax–Vmin)/L; nb = log₂(L) bits/sample
量化误差：–Δ/2 ≤ error ≤ Δ/2Quantization error: –Δ/2 ≤ error ≤ Δ/2
第3步：编码 — 每个样本转换为nb位码字Step 3: Encoding — each sample → nb-bit code word
PCM比特率 = fs × nbPCM bit rate = fs × nb
PCM信噪比：SNR_dB = 6.02·nb + 1.76 dBPCM SNR: SNR_dB = 6.02·nb + 1.76 dB
非均匀量化(压扩) — 低幅度区间更小，保持信噪比恒定Companding — logarithmic spacing, constant SNR across levels
Delta调制 Delta Modulation (DM)
DM：记录与前一个样本的变化（1bit：上升/下降）DM: records change from previous sample (1 bit: up/down)
DPCM：对差值进行多比特量化，精度更高DPCM: quantize the difference with multiple bits, higher accuracy
线路编码 Line Coding
单极性NRZ：有直流分量，无同步Unipolar NRZ: DC component, no synchronization
极性NRZ-L/I：正负电平，NRZ-I遇1翻转Polar NRZ-L/I: positive/negative levels; NRZ-I inverts on 1
RZ归零码：每比特中间归零，解决同步但带宽翻倍RZ: return to zero mid-bit, solves sync but doubles BW
曼彻斯特编码：中间跳变提供同步和数据，用于以太网Manchester: mid-bit transition, used in Ethernet (IEEE 802.3)
差分曼彻斯特：中间跳变仅用于同步；比特开始处有无跳变表示数据Differential Manchester: mid-bit for sync only; start transition encodes data
双极性AMI：1交替±V，0为0V，无直流分量Bipolar-AMI: 1 alternates ±V, 0 is 0V, no DC
伪三进制：1为0V，0交替±VPseudoternary: 1 is 0V, 0 alternates ±V
块编码 Block Coding
4B/5B：4位数据→5位码字（20%开销），配合NRZ-I消除长串零4B/5B: 4-bit data → 5-bit code (20% overhead), eliminates long zero runs
传输模式 Transmission Modes
并行传输：n位同时通过n根线传输，快但成本高Parallel: n bits simultaneously over n wires; fast but costly
串行传输：逐位传输，单通道，成本低Serial: one bit at a time; single channel; cost-effective
异步：每字节加起始位(0)和停止位(1)，适合低速Asynchronous: start/stop bits per byte; ideal for low-speed
同步：数据组合成帧，无起始/停止位，高速Synchronous: frames, no start/stop bits; high-speed
等时：恒定速率，用于实时音视频Isochronous: constant rate for real-time audio/video
量化步长
$$\Delta = \frac{V_{\max} - V_{\min}}{L}$$
每样本比特数
$$n_b = \log_2(L)$$
PCM比特率
$$\text{Bit rate} = f_s \times n_b$$
PCM SNR
$$\text{SNR}_{\text{dB}} = 6.02n_b + 1.76\ \text{dB}$$
奈奎斯特采样
$$f_s \ge 2 f_{\max}$$
NRZ波特率
$$S = \frac{N}{2}\ \text{Baud}$$

📻 广播与多址接入

Broadcasting & Multiple Access
📖 概览 Overview

了解广播技术的发展历史、AM/FM模拟广播、数字音频广播（DAB）以及移动网络中的多址接入协议（FDMA/TDMA/CDMA）。

Explore broadcasting history, AM/FM analog broadcasting, digital audio broadcasting (DAB), and multiple access protocols in mobile networks (FDMA/TDMA/CDMA).

广播历史 History of Broadcasting
1901年：马可尼首次跨大西洋无线电传输（莫尔斯电码）1901: Marconi's first transatlantic radio signal (Morse code)
1920年代：AM广播进入家庭，开启「广播黄金时代」1920s: AM broadcasting becomes household medium — "Golden Age of Radio"
1933年：阿姆斯特朗发明FM，消除AM的静态干扰问题1933: Armstrong invents FM, eliminates AM's static interference
FM优势：更高音质、无静态干扰、低成本FM advantages: better quality, no static, low cost
AM与FM广播 AM & FM Broadcasting
AM：载波530–1700 kHz，每站10 kHz，需保护带AM: carrier 530–1700 kHz, 10 kHz/station, guard bands
FM：载波88–108 MHz，每站200 kHzFM: carrier 88–108 MHz, 200 kHz/station
FM在2000年后已比AM更受欢迎FM became more popular than AM after 2000
数字音频 Digital Audio
CD标准：44.1 kHz采样，16bit量化，双声道 → 1.41 MbpsCD standard: 44.1 kHz, 16-bit, 2 channels → 1.41 Mbps
数字音频优势：无累积噪声/失真，可使用信道编码纠错Digital audio advantage: no accumulated noise/distortion, error correction
DAB 数字音频广播：抗噪声和多径衰落能力更强DAB: more robust against noise and multipath fading
DAB频谱效率约为FM的15倍DAB is ~15× more efficient than FM in spectrum use
多址接入协议 Multiple Access Protocols
